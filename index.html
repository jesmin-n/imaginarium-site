About the Project

This is the next coolest thing ever.

Have you ever wished you could play with the objects of your imagination? Well, you can with Imaginarium. Speak as if you would normally tell a story to a friend, and see it all come to life around you.

The Features

Speak your mind.

We use natural language processing and understanding to perform a 'lil magic on your speech, bringing your words to life.

Multiplayer and cross-platform

Join your friends in Imaginarium, whether they're on the Oculus platform or using the HTC Vive.

Interact with your world.

Pick up, throw, and edit the objects that you create. And import your own assets to personalize the experience as your own.

Record and share your stories.

Save your pieces of oratory brilliance, and share them with friends to replay the magic.
